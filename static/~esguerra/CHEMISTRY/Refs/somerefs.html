New Perspectives in electricity / electromagnetism / electrochemistry?

In this area I would't know what to recommend myself. I mean, perhaps what I can recommend is books that deal with electrostatics applied to molecules, so, it's pure physics applied to chemistry, or perhaps just pure physics, many times the chemistry gets lost in this area. Basically what everyone does in this area is to solve two well know physics equations, these are, the Poisson equation and the Poisson-Boltzmann equation.

Maybe the following review can be good, although it gets a bit elaborate.

Curr Opin Struct Biol. 2006 Apr;16(2):142-51; Koehl P. Electrostatics calculations: Latest methodological advances.
