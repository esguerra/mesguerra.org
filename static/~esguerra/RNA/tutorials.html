What is persistence lenght?
Why is this concept useful and how is it interpreted.
Persistance length is the average of the scalar projections of all bond vectors in a polymeric chain, in a reference bond i, (Migth be thougth as a middle bond?) except the head and tail vectors.(Is it the component of the projection vector?)
There are also types of persistence length, that is:
Persistance Length for Bending
